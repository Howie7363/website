    Contribution Tiers

    If your donation amount falls into any of the ranges below, you can receive the recognition associated with that tier in addition to that of the lower tiers. Please contact us if you made a substantial contribution and wish to remain anonymous or not receive recognition.

    • Gold (greater than $1000): The name of you or your company is placed on our robot.
    • Silver ($500-$1000): The name of you or your company is placed on our team T-shirt.
    • Bronze ($200-$500): The name of you or your company is placed on our website.
